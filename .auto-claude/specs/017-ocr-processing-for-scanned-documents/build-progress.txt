## 2026-04-11 16:00 - Subtask 3-2 Completed: Aggregate Confidence Score Calculation

**What was done:**
- Added `calculate_aggregate_confidence()` method to PDFExtractor class
- Implements weighted average confidence calculation for entire documents
- Formula: (sum of OCR page confidences + text_page_count * 1.0) / total_pages
- Confidence calculation logic:
  - Text-based pages (with extractable text layer) have implicit confidence = 1.0
  - OCR-processed pages use Tesseract confidence scores (0.0 - 1.0)
  - Weighted average accounts for both proportion and quality of OCR pages
- Returns float between 0.0 and 1.0 representing document quality:
  - 1.0 = All pages are text-based (no OCR needed)
  - 0.85+ = Good quality (mostly text or high-quality OCR)
  - 0.7-0.85 = Acceptable quality (some low-quality OCR)
  - <0.7 = Poor quality (many scanned pages with low OCR confidence)
- Edge case handling:
  - Returns 0.0 for documents with 0 pages
  - Clamps result to valid range [0.0, 1.0]
  - Handles division by zero for OCR average logging
